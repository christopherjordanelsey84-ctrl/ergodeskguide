## The Reasoning

### The Desk
Most of your budget goes to the desk. A good standing desk is the foundation — everything else can be upgraded later, but a wobbly desk ruins everything.

We recommend the **FLEXIMATES** over cheaper options because it has genuine motorised height adjustment, is stable at full height, and comes with a proper warranty.

### The Chair
This is where people overspend. The **Hbada Task Chair** at ~£120 genuinely delivers 80% of what a £1,200 Herman Miller gives you. Adjustable lumbar, adjustable armrests, breathable mesh back. 

If your budget stretches, upgrade here first.

### The Monitor Arm
Your monitor is probably at the wrong height. An arm fixes that instantly. The **Ergotron HX** is the gold standard used in professional offices — it moves like butter and lasts forever. Look for the refurbished option on Amazon for ~£120 less.

### Lighting
The most overlooked element. A **BenQ ScreenBar** sits on top of your monitor and lights your desk without screen glare. If you work evenings, this alone will reduce eye strain dramatically.

---

## Setup Tips

1. **Monitor height** — Top of the screen should be at eye level. If you wear glasses, drop it slightly.
2. **Keyboard height** — Elbows at 90 degrees. If you're using a standing desk, be ready to adjust frequently.
3. **Mouse position** — Same level as keyboard, as close as possible.
4. **Standing sessions** — Start with 20 minutes standing per hour. Set a timer. Your legs will thank you.

---

## What to Buy First

If you can only afford one thing: **the monitor arm**. It solves the most common posture problem (screen too low) and costs less than the chair.

If you can only afford two things: **monitor arm + chair**.
