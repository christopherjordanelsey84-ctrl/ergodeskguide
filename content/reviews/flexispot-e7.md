# FlexiSpot E7 Review

The FlexiSpot E7 is the desk that most "best standing desk" lists agree on. But does it actually live up to the hype?

**Short answer:** Yes — mostly.

---

## Specs at a Glance

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Motorised electric |
| **Height range** | 60–125cm |
| **Weight capacity** | 125kg |
| **Motors** | Dual |
| **Memory presets** | 4 |
| **Warranty** | 10 years (frame), 5 years (motor) |
| **Price** | ~£500 |

---

## What We Like

### Stability
At full height (125cm), the E7 is genuinely rock solid. No wobble, even with two monitors and a full PC setup. This is the critical test for any standing desk, and the E7 passes comfortably.

### Motor smoothness
The dual motors deliver smooth, quiet height transitions. At normal speed it's about 38mm/s — not the fastest, but you won't notice. The anti-collision detection is a nice safety feature.

### Weight capacity
125kg is class-leading. Most competitors sit at 70-80kg. If you have heavy equipment or dual monitors with a PC tower on the desk, the E7 handles it without straining.

### Memory presets
Four programmable heights means you can set it for sitting, standing, and two custom positions. Useful if multiple people share the desk.

---

## What We Don't Like

### Assembly
The E7 comes in two boxes and assembly takes 45-60 minutes. The instructions are functional but basic. A second pair of hands helps.

### Controller
TheLED display and touch controls work fine, but the interface is dated. It's not a dealbreaker, but competitors like Uplift have nicer controllers.

### Price creep
The base price is ~£500, but the desktop (if buying separately) adds another £150-200. The all-in price can creep past £700 with accessories.

---

## Who It's For

The FlexiSpot E7 is the desk to buy if you want the best combination of stability, features, and build quality at around the £500 mark. It's not the cheapest, but it is genuinely the best in its class.

If your budget is under £350, look at the FLEXIMATES instead — it's 80% as good for significantly less.
