# Affiliate Disclosure

ErgoDesk Guide is a participant in various affiliate advertising programmes designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising commissions by advertising and linking to products on retailer sites.

## How It Works

When you click on certain links on ErgoDesk Guide and buy something, we may earn a small commission from that sale — at **no extra cost to you**. 

We only recommend products we have researched and genuinely believe in. Our recommendations are never influenced by commission rates or affiliate partnerships.

## What This Means For You

- **You pay nothing extra** — affiliate links don't increase your price
- **We only profit if you buy** — we earn nothing if you just click a link
- **Our opinions are independent** — we rank products by quality, not by commission rate

## Why Support This Model?

Running a detailed review site takes real time and money. The affiliate model lets us operate without charging you subscription fees or showing intrusive ads.
