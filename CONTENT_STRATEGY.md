# ErgoDesk Guide — Content Strategy

## Mission
Build a content site that ranks on Google for ergonomic home office keywords and converts visitors into affiliate revenue through honest, expert reviews.

## Niche
**Ergonomic home office equipment** — standing desks, ergonomic chairs, monitor arms, desk accessories, and lighting.

Target audience: Remote workers and home office setuppers in the UK and US, aged 25-55, with mid-range budgets (£100-£1,000 per setup).

## Revenue Model
1. **Affiliate commissions** — Product links to Amazon UK, Amazon US, FlexiSpot, and other affiliate programmes
2. **Display advertising** — Minimal, non-intrusive (only if affiliate income plateaus)
3. **Info products** — Potential for an ebook ("The Complete Home Office Guide") later

## Content Pillars

### Pillar 1: Best Of Lists
High-traffic, commercial intent. Examples:
- "Best Standing Desks Under £500"
- "Best Ergonomic Chairs 2026"
- "Best Monitor Arms Under £100"

**SEO goal:** Rank for "best X" keywords. Low competition, high commercial intent.

### Pillar 2: Product Reviews
Detailed, honest reviews with scored ratings. Examples:
- FlexiSpot E7 Review
- Herman Miller Aeron Review
- BenQ ScreenBar Review

**SEO goal:** Rank for "[product name] review" keywords.

### Pillar 3: How-To Guides
Educational content that builds topical authority. Examples:
- "How to Set Up an Ergonomic Desk"
- "Standing Desk Exercises to Do While You Work"
- "How to Fix Lower Back Pain at Your Desk"

**SEO goal:** Rank for long-tail informational queries, build topical authority.

### Pillar 4: Comparison Articles
vs-style articles for high-intent buyers. Examples:
- "FlexiSpot E7 vs IKEA BEKANT"
- "Herman Miller Aeron vs Steelcase Gesture"
- "Standing Desk Riser vs Full Standing Desk"

**SEO goal:** Capture pre-purchase research searches.
