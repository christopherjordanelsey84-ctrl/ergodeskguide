March 28, 2026 · ErgoDesk Guide

Privacy Policy#

ErgoDesk Guide is owned and operated by ErgoDesk Guide Ltd (“we”, “us”, “our”).

What Data We Collect#

We do not run analytics trackers (no Google Analytics, no Facebook Pixel, no Hotjar). We believe your browsing habits are your own business.

The only data we collect is:

  • Email addresses — if you subscribe to our newsletter (via a GDPR-compliant provider)
  • Comments — if you leave a comment (name, email, comment text)

How We Use Data#

  • Email addresses are used solely to send our newsletter. You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • Comment data is retained indefinitely unless you request deletion.
  • We never sell or share your data with third parties.

Cookies#

ErgoDesk Guide uses minimal cookies — only:

  • Session cookies for website functionality
  • Optional newsletter subscription cookies

We do not use advertising cookies or tracking cookies.
